Financial notes for Aurora Retail Systems.

Useful facts:
- Annual recurring revenue: $42.0M.
- ARR growth: 31% year over year.
- Net revenue retention: 118%.
- Gross revenue retention: 89%.
- Cloud product gross margin: 71%.
- Blended gross margin: 63%.
- Professional services revenue: 27% of total revenue.
- Cash balance: $18.5M.
- Burn multiple: 1.6x.
- Sales payback: 14 months.

Conflicting signal:
- Management calls gross margin "best in class for our segment," but blended gross margin is dragged down by services and three single-tenant deployments.
- Finance projects services revenue will fall to 17% of total revenue next year, but the services backlog is currently at a 9-month high.

Risk details:
- Three single-tenant customers represent 21% of revenue and materially reduce gross margin.
- If two of the three customers migrate successfully, blended gross margin could move from 63% to roughly 68%.
- If migration fails, infrastructure cost and support load remain elevated.
- Customer acquisition cost increased 19% because enterprise sales cycles lengthened.
