Legal and contract notes for Aurora Retail Systems.

Useful facts:
- Standard contract term is 24 months.
- Enterprise contracts include uptime commitments.
- Two large customers negotiated custom data residency clauses.
- Three customers still require single-tenant infrastructure under old agreements.
- New cloud contracts move customers to shared infrastructure by default.
- Termination for convenience exists in 11% of contracts, mostly smaller retailers.

Contract risk:
- Custom data residency clauses may slow migration for international customers.
- Single-tenant commitments are the main legal reason infrastructure cost remains high.
- Some old contracts require 180 days notice before architecture changes.
- Legal believes two of the three single-tenant customers can be migrated at renewal.

Compliance notes:
- SOC 2 Type II report is available.
- ISO 27001 audit is in progress.
- Data processing agreements are updated for new cloud contracts.
- One European customer requested extra data localization assurances.
