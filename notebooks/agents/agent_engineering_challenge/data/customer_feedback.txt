Customer feedback export for Aurora Retail Systems.

Positive feedback:
- Enterprise customer Northline Stores says cloud reporting is faster and easier to share with regional managers.
- MetroMart expanded from inventory management to loyalty analytics after migration.
- Three cloud-migrated customers increased event volume by more than 40%.
- Users like the new exception dashboard because store managers can investigate stockouts without filing tickets.

Negative feedback:
- Smaller retailers say migration documentation assumes too much IT knowledge.
- Several customers report confusion around POS connector versions.
- Two customers say support response time slowed during migration waves.
- One customer says a failed connector test delayed a planned store rollout by four weeks.

Support ticket themes:
- POS connector setup.
- Data import mapping.
- Loyalty module configuration.
- Permissions for regional managers.
- Slow first sync after migration.

Useful synthesis:

Analyst note:
Customer feedback supports a "painful migration, valuable destination" thesis. This is attractive if onboarding friction is temporary and dangerous if services/support remain permanently high.
